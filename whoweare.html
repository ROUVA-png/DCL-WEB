Who We Are

Deus Cautela Limited is a leading private security company in Nigeria with over 30 years of dedicated service. Our mission is to safeguard people, assets, and organizations with professional precision and unwavering commitment.

We combine human intelligence, modern technology, and tested expertise to deliver security solutions that build trust and ensure peace of mind.

Our Mission

To provide security services at the level that allows our clients to go about their activities and business with peace of mind and in a climate of safety while achieving their business objectives.

Our Vision

To be one of the best commercial / Industrial / Personnel security services companies in nigeria, and to be a reference point for others in the industry, both in africa and the rest of the world while holding onto our "Operation Performance excellence"
