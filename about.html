Who We Are

Deus Cautela Limited (DCL) is a licensed and registered private security firm (RC: 1303572), established in December 2015. Driven by a seasoned board and management team with decades of combined experience, we provide comprehensive security services—uniformed guards, executive protection, electronic security, and training—tailored to industrial and commercial clients.

Mission & Vision

Vision: To be one of the best commercial / Industrial / Personnel security services companies in nigeria, and to be a reference point for others in the industry, both in africa and the rest of the world while holding onto our "Operation Performance excellence"

Mission: To provide security services at the level that allows our clients to go about their activities and business with peace of mind and in a climate of safety while achieving their business objectives.
